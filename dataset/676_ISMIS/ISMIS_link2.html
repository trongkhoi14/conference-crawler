Welcome to ISMIS 2024

ISMIS is an established and prestigious conference for exchanging the latest research results in building intelligent systems. It provides a basis for exchanging research results and transferring scientific achievements towards industrial and socio-economic applications. The scope of ISMIS is to present a wide range of topics related to the application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques related to areas such as decision support, knowledge-based systems, machine learning, robotics, planning, computer vision, databases, information retrieval, configuration, and diagnosis. The conference also focuses on interdisciplinary research in AI-related fields, for example, decision support systems, human decision-making, recommender systems and personalization, knowledge-based systems development, cognitive aspects of knowledge understanding.

This website is dedicated to the registration process of the ISMIS conference. Please visit the full website for complete details: https://ismis2024.ensma.fr

Registration

  • All prices are listed in EURO and include French V.A.T (20%). To view the current foreign exchange rates, please visit European Central Bank.
  • The ISMIS 2024 conference registration includes: participation to all sessions of all tracks and all events, the Industry Session, lunches and coffee breaks, ticket to the social events and conference material.
  • Each accepted paper must be presented by at least one author, otherwise, the paper will be removed from the conference proceedings and program.
  • Each accepted paper must have at least one full registration for it to be included in the proceedings.
  • To be eligible for the student discount, you must have been a student on an accredited institute the day of the submission deadline. Documentation proving registered student status (student’s ID Card or a letter from the Head of Department/Director of Doctoral School prepared on the notepaper of the institution) should be sent to: ismis2024@ensma.fr.
  • Cancellations received after the 7th of June will be not refunded except in specific circumstances. Please contact the organizing committee for further assistance.
  • Due to space limitations in the Proceedings, the camera-ready versions of accepted papers are limited to 10 pages for Regular Papers and 8 pages for Short Paper. If absolutely needed, the number of pages may be increased up to a maximum of 12 (Regular Papers) and 9 (Short Papers). However, for each extra page, the author will have to pay an additional fee of 100 EUR.
  • The costs for accompanying persons include (each 150 EUR): lunches and ticket to the social events (welcome reception and social dinner).

To proceed with the online form submission and online payment: https://ismis2024.sciencesconf.org/registration
