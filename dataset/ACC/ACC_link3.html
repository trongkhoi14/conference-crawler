45ACC

December 11, 2023 - December 15, 2023

The 45th Australasian Combinatorics Conference (45ACC)

Dates: 11–15 December, 2023
Venue: University of Western Australia, Perth

ACC is the annual conference of the Combinatorial Mathematics Society of Australasia. The conference covers all areas of combinatorics in mathematics and computer science.

** Registration is now open, as is abstract submission. **

Invited speakers

  • Sara Davies (The University of Queensland)
  • Gary Greaves (Nanyang Technical University)
  • Krystal Guo (University of Amsterdam)
  • André Kündgen (California State University San Marcos)
  • Padraig Ó Cathláin (Dublin City University)
  • Tibor Szabó (Freie Universität Berlin)
  • Geertrui Van de Voorde (University of Canterbury)
  • Gabriel Verret (University of Auckland)

Attendees are invited to give contributed talks. Contributed talks will be around 20–25 minutes in length including time for questions.

The CMSA Anne Penfold Street Student Prize will be awarded to the best student talk at the conference.

Key dates

  • Early bird registration ends: 6 November 2023
  • Abstract submission ends: 27 November 2023

For more information see https://45acc.github.io or contact the organisers at 45ACCuwa@gmail.com.
