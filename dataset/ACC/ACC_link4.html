43rd Australasian Combinatorics Conference

13–17 December 2021, The University of Melbourne, Australia

Notices and news

Due to COVID-19 and related restrictions, this conference was postponed from 14–18 December 2020 to 13–17 December 2021. More than 130 participants from 26 countries have registered the conference, and there will have 80 talks at the conference, including 10 invited lectures. The conference will be running in Zoom meetings.

December 8, 2021

About

ACC is the annual conference of the Combinatorial Mathematics Society of Australasia (CMSA). It covers all areas of combinatorics in mathematics and computer science.

This conference is previously known as ACCMCC (Australasian Conference on Combinatorial Mathematics and Combinatorial Computing), and the name is changed to ACC from 2020 onwards following a recommendation of CMSA. Past and future editions of ACCMCC/ACC can be found here.

The 43rd ACC will be hosted by The University of Melbourne, Australia, 13-17 December 2021.
