AMIA 2024 Annual Symposium Sponsorship Information

With more than 2,300 health and healthcare informatics professionals and students in attendance, the AMIA Annual Symposium is the world’s premier meeting for the research and practice of biomedical and health informatics.

From informative presentations and insightful panels to engaging posters, dynamic plenaries, inspiring keynotes, an expansive exhibition, interactive roundtables, special events, and ample networking opportunities — the AMIA 2024 Annual Symposium offers a seamless platform to connect with leading researchers, innovators, and practitioners in informatics.
