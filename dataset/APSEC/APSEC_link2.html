Call for Submissions: Doctoral Symposium

The APSEC 2023 Doctoral Symposium is an opportunity for PhD students in the field of software engineering to present and discuss their work with other researchers and practitioners. The symposium aims to provide a venue for doctoral students to receive feedback from academics and industry experts on their research, and to network with other students and professionals in the field.

The Doctoral Symposium will be a half-day event where all attendees will present their work by focusing on one of their open research questions. There will be appropriate questions, discussions, and audience feedback after the presentations.

Submission Categories:

We invite submissions from PhD students in two categories:

  • Early Doctoral Track: This track is for students in the early stages of their PhD, who have already settled on a research area but have no publications that are core to their PhD and who are looking for early feedback on how to continue their PhD.
  • Late Doctoral Track: These are aimed at students who already have results that they wish to present at the symposium.

Submission Instructions

Each Doctoral Symposium submission consists of two parts:

  1. A short paper. This paper submission has to be authored by the student only. The Doctoral Symposium does NOT require submissions to be double-anonymized for the review.
  2. A letter of recommendation from a student’s dissertation advisor. The letter must include student’s name, an assessment of the current status of your dissertation research, and an expected date for dissertation submission, The letter should directly come from the student’s advisor and be sent in PDF format to the Doctoral Symposium Chair (Yunho Kim, yunhokim@hanyang.ac.kr) with the subject: APSEC 2023 Doctoral Symposium Support Letter for [student name]

All submissions must be in English, come in A4 paper size PDF format, and conform, at the time of submission, to the IEEE Conference Proceedings Formatting Guidelines (title in 24pt font and full text in 10pt font; LaTeX users must use \documentclass[10pt,conference]{IEEEtran} without including the compsoc or compsocconf option). Submissions must be submitted electronically in PDF before the due date via EasyChair.

The Chair reserves the right to reject submissions (without reviews) that are not in compliance or are out of scope for the conference.

Submission Link

Papers must be submitted through https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=apsec2023 and recommendation letters should directly come from the student’s advisor and be sent in PDF format to the Doctoral Symposium Chair (Yunho Kim, yunhokim@hanyang.ac.kr) with the subject: APSEC 2023 Doctoral Symposium Support Letter for [student name]

Evaluation Criteria

Submissions will be reviewed by the Doctoral Symposium Committee. The participants will be selected based on their anticipated contribution to the goals of the symposium and the potential benefits to the participants. The review process will consider the potential quality of the research, its relevance to software engineering, the stage of the research, and the diversity of backgrounds, research topics, and approaches of the submissions.
