APCCM 2018 14th Asia-Pacific Conference on Conceptual Modelling

29 January - 02 February 2018 / Brisbane,  QLD,  Australia 

About APCCM 2018

  • 2018-01-29 12:00:00 2018-02-02 12:00:00 Europe/London APCCM 2018 14th Asia-Pacific Conference on Conceptual Modelling University of Queensland Eventegg info@eventegg.com

14th Asia-Pacific Conference on Conceptual Modelling, will be held from 29 Jan to 02 Feb 2018.

14th Asia-Pacific Conference on Conceptual Modelling will take place at the University of Queensland in Brisbane, Queensland Australia.

Bringing industry and academia together, 14th Asia-Pacific Conference on Conceptual Modelling is a 5 days event on Computing, Software, Information Security, Information Technologies, Computer Sciences and Conceptual Modelling fields. It also suggests you the opportunity for face-to-face cooperation with those leading the future.

APCCM 2018 is organized annually.

The Conference is brought to you by Computing Research and Education Association of Australasia.
