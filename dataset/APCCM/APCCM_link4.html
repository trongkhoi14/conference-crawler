Welcome to the 4th Asia-Pacific Conference on Physical Modelling in Geotechnics
ACPMG 2024

11 – 13 December 2024
Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates

New York University in Abu Dhabi (NYUAD) is delighted to welcome you to the 4th Asia-Pacific Conference on Physical Modelling in Geotechnics (ACPMG 2024) in the dynamic city of Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates, from 11th to 13th December 2024.

The conference aims to provide an up-to-date overview of the latest developments in multi-scale physical modelling within the following themes:

  • New facilities, new equipment, and measuring techniques
  • Resilient Geotechnical Infrastructure
  • Sustainability in Geotechnical Systems
  • Energy geo-structures and foundation systems
  • Application of Machine Learning/Artificial Intelligence in Geotechnical Engineering 

The three-day conference will take place on the NYUAD campus, which is about 20-minutes drive from Abu Dhabi city center.

We look forward to welcoming you to Abu Dhabi in December 2024!

Organising Committee

ACPMG 2024 is organised by the Technical Committee on Physical Modelling (TC104) of the International Society of Soil Mechanics and Geotechnical Engineering (ISSMGE).
