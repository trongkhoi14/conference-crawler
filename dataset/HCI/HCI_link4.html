Doctoral Consortium

Take part in the Doctoral Consortium at the 37th International BCS Human-Computer Interaction Conference. The Doctoral Consortium will be chaired by Professor Raymond Bond and Professor Janet Read.

Date: 15th July 2024 (likely all day)
Location: University of Central Lancashire, Preston

This is an opportunity to present and discuss your PhD research project with your peers and get feedback from experts in human-computer interaction.

Call for Doctoral Consortium paper submissions:

Paper Submission Deadline: 30th April 2024

Please submit a 2-page paper using the eWiC template available here which can be co-authored with your collaborators and supervisors.

Submit your paper via Microsoft CMT here (if you don’t have an account you can follow these instructions to create one).

The 2-page paper should describe the background to your project along with the aim, objectives and research questions/hypotheses in your PhD project. You should also present current protocols and study designs that you intend on using. Do also consider the implications and potential impact of answering the research questions in your PhD.
