REGISTRATION

CAADRIA 2024 will be organized in a physical conference format. All paper presentations will be held in person. All associated other programmes, such as keynotes, and panel discussions, will be held in person. Some workshops, industry events, and exhibitions will run as in-person events. Please indicate within your registration if you will attend the indicated activities.

The registration fees represent the significant efforts that are required to organize the conference, which results in the following benefits for participants:

  • CAADRIA Membership 2024-2025
  • a digital copy of the conference proceedings
  • access to all events, including workshops, keynote lectures, roundtable discussions, networking sessions and tours;
  • for authors: a rigorous, two stage peer-review process offering valuable feedback on your research paper;
  • for accepted authors: inclusion in the conference presentation and sharing sessions, discussing your work with peers and experts in your field;
  • for accepted authors: inclusion of your paper in conference proceedings, which will be made available both online and in research libraries around the world and will be indexed in CUMINCAD, a cumulative index of publications about computer aided architectural design;

During the registration, we strongly encourage all attendees to be physically present in Singapore. We will plan social events according to the number of national and international visitors. If you need a confirmation of attendance for visa or funding reasons please get in contact caadria2024@sutd.edu.sg. We will send you a letter confirming that you are attending the conference. Please state your name, email, and paper ID.

terms & Conditions

  • No refund is given for cancellation of any reason.
  • Audiences registering may input ‘NA’ on spaces that are not applicable.
  • Presenters who are students may register under the student pricing.
  • Presenters who are non-students may register under the regular pricing.
  • Registrations cannot be transferred to another person.
  • A valid student ID is required to be uploaded during payment for student registration.
  • To be eligible for publication, each paper requires one conference registration for the presenter. Multiple submissions by the same author are subject to multiple registrations.
  • All prices are inclusive of Gala dinner and post Conference events.
  • Gala dinner only tickets are single tickets issued to those who are not attending the conference.
  • Regular tickets also refers to attendees who are not presenting.
  • Should you require financial help, please do not hesitate to contact the organizing committee at caadria2024@sutd.edu.sg

REGISTERING AS AN ORGANISATION?

If you are registering for an organisation of 5 or more people, contact caadria2024@sutd.edu.sg for corporate discount and benefits.

Personal Information Collection   Statement

The personal data collected will be used by The CAADRIA 2024 Organizing Committee for processing the captioned event. The Organizers may send you news and follow-up emails. All personal data you provided will not be disclosed to any third party.

VISA

If you need a confirmation of attendance for visa or funding reasons please get in contact caadria2024@sutd.edu.sg. We will send you a letter confirming that you are attending the conference. Please state your name, email, and paper ID.

CPD POINTS

3 CPD points will be awarded to registered architects in Singapore.

As a multiple day event, please take note that architects that require CPD points must attend all conference proper days in order to be qualified for the CPD points. 

For further enquires, please get in contact with caadria2024@sutd.edu.sg.
