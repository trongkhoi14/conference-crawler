CoNEXT 2023 – International Conference on emerging Networking EXperiments and Technologies

The ROC team hosts and coorganizes with Huawei France the ACM CoNEXT 2023 conference, an important young event of the ACM Special Interest Group on Communications (SIGCOMM).

The conference will run from Dec. 5 to Dec. 8 in Cnam main campus at 292 rue Saint Martin, 3ème arondissement, Paris.

Our master, Ph.D. students and research staff will do their best to make this event a good memory for the community. More info : https://conferences.sigcomm.org/co-next/2023/#!/home
