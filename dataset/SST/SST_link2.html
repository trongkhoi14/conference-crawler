Forthcoming Conference dates

The conference is nearly always in the week before Holy Week or the week after Easter Week (Western calendar). This sometimes needs to be changed, however, so always check the actual dates advertised in any given year. The confirmed dates for the forthcoming conferences can be found below. These conferences will all take place at the University of Warwick.

Easter Conference
2023  9 April 17-19 April
2024 31 March 8-10 April
2025 20 April 7-9 April
2026 5 April 13-15 April
2027 TBD TBD
2028 TBD TBD

The general rule is: if Easter Sunday falls on or before 7 April, the conference is in the week after Easter Week; if Easter Sunday falls on 8 April or later, the conference is in the week before Holy Week. This is dependent on availability at host venues so do ensure you check dates before booking.
