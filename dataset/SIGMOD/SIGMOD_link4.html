CANCELLATION POLICY

In case of any cancellation or non-attendance, unfortunately, the registration fees are not refundable.


PAYMENT METHODS

Pay online with a credit card via PayPal for either your conference or workshop registration. Alternatively, you can pay for your conference registration or workshop fees through a bank transfer. It is mandatory to select wire transfer payment terms as 'OUR', which means all transfer costs must be borne by the attendee.
