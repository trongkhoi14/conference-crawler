SIGMOD/PODS 2025 Anti-Harassment Policy

SIGMOD 2025 adheres to the ACM Policy Against Harassment. Any participant who experiences unacceptable behavior may contact any current member of the SIGMOD Executive Committee and the PODS Executive Committee or DBCares. Please be assured that if you approach us, your concerns will be kept in strict confidence, and we will consult with you on any actions taken.
