Welcome to MobiHoc 2024

MobiHoc 2024, the 25th International Symposium on Theory, Algorithmic Foundations, and Protocol Design for Mobile Networks and Mobile Computing will be held in Athens, Greece, October 14-17, 2024. MobiHoc is a premier international symposium dedicated to addressing challenges in dynamic networks and computing.

It will bring together researchers and practitioners from a broad spectrum of networking research to present the most up-to-date results and achievements in the field.

MobiHoc 2024 will feature a highly selective technical program, multiple distinguished keynote addresses, and an exciting panel. In addition, it includes workshops that are focused on areas of emerging interest.
