About the Conference

The Annual International Conference on Privacy, Security & Trust (PST) provides a premier forum for sharing advances in cybersecurity research and security applications. PST2024 will be held in person in Sydney, Australia, and will offer three days of keynotes, technical presentations, posters, special sessions, and an Industrial day with vendor exhibition.

Organized by the University of New South Wales and the Canadian Institute for Cybersecurity
