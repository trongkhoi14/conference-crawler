OzCHI 2024: Beyond Being Human

30th November to 4th December 2024
Meanjin | Brisbane, Australia

Welcome to OzCHI 2024

OzCHI 2024 is the 36th Australian Conference on Human-Computer Interaction. This annual non-profit event is for the Computer-Human Interaction Special Interest Group (CHISIG) and serves as a premier forum for the latest advancements in HCI research and practice. This year's event will be hosted in Brisbane, Australia, from 30 November to 4 December 2024.

OzCHI is Australasia's leading forum for cutting-edge HCI research and practice. It attracts a diverse international community of researchers, industry practitioners, academics, and students. Participants come from over 25 countries and various fields, including interaction design, user experience, information architecture, software engineering, human factors, information systems analysis, and social sciences. We welcome and encourage international and industry submissions and participation.

Theme Beyond Being Human

This year’s conference theme Beyond Being Human reflects the continuing debate about how HCI extends beyond designing with and for humans, to include non-human agents from the natural and artificial realms.

Venue The University of Queensland, St Lucia Campus

The venue for OzCHI 2024 is the University of Queensland’s main campus in St Lucia. The entire conference will take place in the Advanced Engineering Building, which borders the recently renovated UQ Lakes precinct, with the single paper presentation track taking place in the GHD Auditorium.  Brisbane’s integrated public transit system will offer conference participants multiple options for arriving on campus from city centre venues and accommodation, with the CityCat river ferries a popular choice.
