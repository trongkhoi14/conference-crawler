Welcome to ISPR'2024

 

Welcome to Istanbul Cilty, Turkey From 26 to 28 June 2024

We are delighted to announce the 4'th International Conference on Intelligent Systems and Pattern Recognition "ISPR'2024", which will be held in Istanbul, City, Turkey,  June 26-28, 2024. Building on the success of previous conferences three edition helded in Hammamet, Tunisia. This event promises to bring together leading researchers from around the world to share their latest solutions, scientific results, and methods in solving intriguing problems in the field of intelligent systems and pattern recognition.

Our conference offers a unique platform for researchers to connect and discuss cutting-edge developments in a variety of subject areas. These include but are not limited to artificial intelligence, pattern recognition, natural language processing, knowledge representation and reasoning, machine and deep learning, data mining, and computer vision. The conference program will feature keynote speeches by prominent figures in the field, as well as regular paper presentations in parallel tracks.

All papers submitted for consideration will be reviewed by at least three members of our international program committee, with additional reviewers invited as needed. Acceptance will be based on originality, significance, technical soundness, and clarity of presentation.

The proceedings of ISPR'2024 will be published by Springer in their Communications in Computer and Information Science series (CCIS), which is indexed in multiple leading databases.

 

  

 

We are proud to have the support of the International Association for Pattern Recognition (IAPR), which has endorsed this conference since its inception.

In addition, we will be awarding the top three best papers with the "IAPR Best Papers Award." Selected extended versions will be recommended for possible publication in  Q1-ranked  journals , indexed and listed in the leading indexes and databases, including Scopus, DBLP, Web of Science, and ACM Computing Reviews.

Subject areas include all the current fields of interest represented by the Technical Committees of the International Association of Pattern Recognition, and other developing themes involving learning and recognition.
