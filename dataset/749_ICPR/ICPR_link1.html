About Conference

The International Conference on Pattern Recognition (ICPR) is the flagship conference of the International Association of Pattern Recognition and the premier conference in Pattern Recognition, Covering Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Image, Speech, Sensor Pattern Processing etc. ICPR 2024 is the 27th event of the series and it provides a great opportunity to nurture new ideas and collaboration for students, academics, and industry researchers.

The venue of the conference is Biswa Bangla Convention Centre, Kolkata, India.
