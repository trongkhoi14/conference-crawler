Call for Workshop Papers

Workshops will be held in conjunction with the IEEE ANTS 2023 main conference. The intent of the workshops is to cover specialized topics by a combination of regular papers, panel discussions, keynotes, panel discussions, and possibly poster presentations.

IMPORTANT DATES

Workshop Paper Submission Deadline:  7 September 2023 20 October 2023 (Extended)
Workshop Paper Acceptance Notification: 10 November 2023
Camera Ready Paper Due: 2 December 2023

 

The following workshops are open for paper submissions.
