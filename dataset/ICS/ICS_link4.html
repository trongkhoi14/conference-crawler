ICS 2023 Annual Dinner at the Metro Toronto Convention Centre (Meeting Venue)

The ICS 2023 Annual Dinner will be held at the Metro Toronto Convention Centre. This is always a very valuable networking opportunity and great fun!

The event will take place in the Constitution Hall (Room 106), a ballroom where our guests will have the opportunity to meet and appreciate fine cuisine with local and international colleagues. And of course in keeping with ICS tradition, entertainment will be provided.

Tickets are only US $95 to book, either via the online registration system or registered delegates can contact Tsvetina Berova on tberova@kenes.com.

Dress Code: Black Tie Optional, Business Attire Required
