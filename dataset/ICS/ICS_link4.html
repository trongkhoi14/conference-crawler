ICS 2024: International Conference on Supercomputing

June 4-7, 2024

International Conference Hall, Kyoto University, Kyoto, Japan

Conference Venue

International Conference Hall, Kyoto University

Note: The official name of the venue is "International Conference Hall", although Google search will return hits for "International Exchange Hall".



NOTICE

Please note that breakfast is not provided at the conference venue. In Kyoto, many hotels and ryokans are famous for their delicious breakfasts. We encourage you to either choose an accommodation plan that includes breakfast or to explore the city for local breakfast options.

Directions

The conference venue is located in the Yoshida Campus (Buildings numbered 3 at Main Campus) of Kyoto University.
Therefore, you need to go to JR Kyoto Station first, and then take a bus, train or cab to get there.
  1. How to get to JR Kyoto Station

    2. An easy and recommended route to Kyoto Station is to take a limousine bus from Kansai International Airport (KIX) or Osaka International Airport (ITM), as shown below. Of course, there are other ways to get there, so please refer to this page for details.



  2. How to get to the conference venue from JR Kyoto Station

    • Bus: Take the 206 City Bus and get off at the Kyodai Seimon-mae (Kyoto Univerisity) stop. From there, it is a five-minute walk. For directions, please refer to this page.

    • Train: The nearest station to the venue (15 min. on foot) is Demachiyanagi. See the route.

    • Taxi: Uber is available in Kyoto.
