Call For Papers

Theme:

The Future of Artificial Intelligence: Challenges and its Impact on Education, Economics, and Business

Sub Themes:

ACoMC 2023 conference is a premier platform for the presentation of novel and creative research results in the fields of theoretical and applied Management, Information Technology, and Global Risk Impacts on Economics, Business, and Education practices and sciences. The conference will bring together leading researchers, scientists, and students in the domain of scientific interest from around the world.  The areas of interest for submission include, but are not limited to:
  • Arts
  • Social Science
  • Economics
  • Business Administration
  • Accounting
  • Office Administration
  • Humanities
  • Literature
  • Applied Science
  • Finance
  • Education
  • Sports
  • Social Responsibility
  • History
  • Religious Studies
  • Islamic Banking
  • Islamic Finance
  • Law
  • Communication
  • Politics
  • Social and Cultural Issues
  • Travel/Transportation/Tourism
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Other related topics

Publication Opportunities:

Selected articles can be published in the following journals (subject to additional requirements - click to visit the journal's page):

  1. Jurnal Economia - SINTA 2 (Publication Fee: IDR 1.000.000)
  2. Efisiensi: Kajian Ilmu Administrasi - SINTA 3 (Publication Fee: IDR 500.000)
  3. Jurnal Ekonomi dan Pendidikan - SINTA 4 (Publication Fee: IDR 0)
  4. Jurnal Ilmu Manajemen - SINTA 5 (Publication Fee: IDR 0)
  5. Nominal Barometer Riset Akuntansi dan Manajemen - SINTA 3 (Publication Fee: IDR 500.000)
  6. New Challenges in Accoounting and Finance - indexCopernicus (€ 120 - € 140 )
  7. European Journal of Studies in Management and Business - indexCopernicus (€ 120 - € 140 )**
  8. Economic and Regional Studies - indexCopernicus (free )**

**) to be confirmed
