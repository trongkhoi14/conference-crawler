Special Issue on “Traffic Noise”

Acoustical Science and Technology (AST) welcomes manuscripts for a special issue on “Traffic Noise”, and we are looking for papers on traffic noise. The Research Committee on Road Traffic Noise in the Acoustical Society of Japan (ASJ) has been conducting research on methods for the prediction and calculation of road traffic noise and publishes a road traffic noise prediction model “ASJ RTN-Model” every five years. The latest version “ASJ RTN-Model 2023” will be published in the Journal of the Acoustical Society of Japan, Vol 80, No 4, and we also plan to publish this English version in the AST.

This special issue covers the research on not only road traffic noise, but also a wide range of traffic noise, such as railway noise, aircraft noise, and so on, using a wide range of methods such as numerical simulation and measurement. We are looking forward to submitting your research.

Contact: Editorial Board of the Acoustical Society of Japan (Tel. 03-5256-1020, e-mail: asj-editcom-jou [at] acoustics.jp)

Journal Name: Acoustical Science and Technology (AST)

Types of Manuscripts Accepted: Paper, Technical Report, Acoustical Letter

Description of Manuscript: Research results related to traffic noise (road traffic, railway, aircraft, and so on)

Submission Deadline: July 1, 2024 (Mon) (September 1, 2024 (Sun) for Acoustical Letters)

Publication Date: July 2025 (AST, Vol. 46, No. 4)

Note: When submitting your paper, please write “Traffic Noise” in the “Special Issue” field of Step 3: Attributes in the web submission system.
