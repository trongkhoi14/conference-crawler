Call for papers

2023-03-03

Aquatic Science and Technology is now inviting submissions for its Vol. 11, No. 1, 2023 issue. All manuscripts should be written in professional English, with a length of 3000-8000 words. The submission should be made in MS-Word format and sent to ast@bigedu.org or submit online. We look forward to receiving your valuable contributions.
