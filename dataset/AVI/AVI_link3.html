Registration

Authors whose works are accepted must formalize their registration before September 07, 2023 in the congress to ensure their inclusion in the conference proceedings, at least one author for each article.

For each registration, a maximum of one article will be published and presented. Above this amount, authors have to pay for additional articles, up to a maximum of one more article.

 

Participation in the XIII International Conference on Virtual Campus will have a cost that will serve to cover the expenses derived from the Conference, whether they choose the face-to-face or the non-face-to-face modality, and the cost will be the same for the 2 modalities.

If this year, finally, the face-to-face modality cannot be carried out due to the displacement problem derived from COVID, the conferences will be held virtually and the cost of registration will be the same as if there were no such circumstances (since this modality also requires costs). On September 07, registrants will be informed if these sessions can finally be done in person for those who have opted for this modality.

The registration fee covers the entrance to the Conference, the possibility of sending an article and presenting it at the Conference, the inclusion of the article in the conference proceedings and its indexing in IEEE (for which the presentation of the article in the Conference) and the membership fee of the Virtual Campus University Network (RUCV) for one year (www.campusvirtuales.net).

 

Modality of participation PRESENTIAL

Those who choose to register for in-person authors must send the article in English through the EasyChair system and send the presentation recorded on video (with a maximum duration of 5 minutes, in Spanish, Portuguese or English) once the article has been accepted. This video will be sent to jicv@uajournals.comThis email address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it. in .avi format before September 07, 2023. The videos will be published on YouTube and will be available through the Conference Program, and the speaker must be present on the day it is played his presentation.

Modality of participation NON-PRESENTIAL

Those who choose to register for non-contact authors must send the article in English through the EasyChair system and send the presentation recorded on video (with a maximum duration of 5 minutes, in Spanish, Portuguese or English) once the article has been accepted. This video will be sent to jicv@uajournals.comThis email address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it. in .avi format before September 07, 2023. The videos will be published on YouTube and will be available through the Conference Program, and the speakers must be present on the day it is played his presentation.

IMPORTANT - BOTH MODALITIES

However, if the face-to-face mode cannot be carried out due to the problem of displacement derived from COVID, the sessions will be held virtually and all those registered (both those who opted for the face-to-face mode and those who opted for the virtual mode) must be present virtually on the day your presentation is played. THIS INFORMATION WILL BE COMMUNICATED TO ALL REGISTERED ON SEPTEMBER 07, 2023.
