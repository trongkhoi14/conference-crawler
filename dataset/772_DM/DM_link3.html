HOUSING ALERT






HOUSING SCAM ALERT
SIAM does not share or distribute personal, registration or housing details with any company or person. Third-party solicitation of hotel reservations is not approved by SIAM, and any claim SIAM has provided attendee contact information for solicitation is fraudulent. Reservations offered by unaffiliated organizations may promise lower rates; however, they may be illegitimate, nonexistent, non-refundable, or have unreasonable cancellation or change penalties. If you book your hotel with a third-party provider, SIAM cannot assist you with any problems incurred as a result of booking outside the official SIAM room block. Please be aware of any unauthorized solicitations and report them to meetings@siam.org.

Transportation Information





 
 
Transportation
 
 
Transportation
 
By Air
 Attendees should plan to fly into Spokane International Airport. Attendees should make reservations directly with their preferred airline, travel agency or travel website. 
 
Ground Transportation 
 
Public Transportation from Airport
 Taxis are available at the airport for transportation to the hotel. Please visit your hotels website to find local directions. Additional ground transportation information is available on the Spokane International Airport website. 
 
By Train
 Amtrak has a rail station in Spokane. To make train reservations through Amtrak, call (800) USA-RAIL, go to http://www.amtrak.com or contact your travel agency or travel website. Taxis are available at the train station for transportation to the hotel. 
 
Rental Cars
 Many major car rental companies have offices inside the airport terminal. You can make your arrangements when you arrive at the airport, or you can make them through your own travel agency or travel website. 
 
Driving Directions
 When using a phone or car GPS system for directions, SIAM highly recommends you contact the hotel directly or visit their website for driving directions. In many cases, the street address of the building and the address required for the GPS are different.
