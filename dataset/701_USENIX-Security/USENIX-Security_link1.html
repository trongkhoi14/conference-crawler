USENIX Security Symposium 2024

  • Security Conferences
  • USENIX Security Symposium 2024
Location: Philadelphia, PA
Date: August 14, 2024
Venue: Philadelphia Downtown Marriot
Organizer: USENIX

The 33rd USENIX Security Symposium, scheduled for August 14–16, 2024, at the Philadelphia Marriott Downtown in Philadelphia, PA, USA, is a premier gathering for researchers, practitioners, system programmers, and enthusiasts invested in the latest developments in computer system and network security and privacy. This symposium serves as a vital platform for sharing cutting-edge research, insights, and practical knowledge aimed at advancing the field. Attendees can expect to engage with a diverse community of experts, participate in informative sessions, and contribute to the ongoing dialogue shaping the future of security and privacy in technology.
