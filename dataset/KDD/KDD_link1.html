Paper Deadlines

Clarification on two paper deadlines:

In 2024 there will be two paper deadlines. Accepted papers submitted by Feb 8, 2024 will appear in the KDD 24 conference and proceedings. Accepted papers submitted by Aug 8, 2024 will appear in the KDD 2025 Conference. The reason to move to 2 deadlines a year is to better accommodate the fast growing field. In parallel to this change, SIGKDD is investigating to make the proceedings a journal so papers will be officially published sooner than the conference date.
