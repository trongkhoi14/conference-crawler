-- ABOUT VECoS --

The VECoS conference series focuses on the analysis of computer
and communication systems, where functional and extra-functional
properties are inter-related. VECoS encourages the cross-fertilization
between the various formal verification and evaluation approaches,
methods and techniques, especially those developed for concurrent
and distributed hardware/software systems. VECoS is referenced by
https://conferences-computer.science/ and ranked in category C by CORE

Topics and more details can be found at http://vecos-world.org/2024/

An overview of previous VECoS conferences can be found at :
http://vecos-world.org/

-- GENERAL INFORMATION ON SUBMISSIONS --

Submitted papers must be no longer than 15 pages (including
bibliography), written in English, presenting original work
and not be submitted for publication elsewhere.  Papers must
be submitted electronically in PDF format through EasyChair
(https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=vecos2024), using the LNCS format
(version 2.20 or above).  Accepted papers will appear in the conference
proceedings.  At least one author of each accepted paper must attend
the conference and present the paper.

The conference proceedings will be published in the Springer-Verlag
Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS) series.

Authors of selected papers will be invited to submit extended
versions for a special issue of selected papers from the Conference
will be considered for publication in extended and revised form
in a special issue of an indexed international journal (ISSE
<http://www.springer.com/computer/swe/journal/11334>:
Innovations in Systems and Software Engineering, IJCCBS
<http://www.inderscience.com/jhome.php?jcode=ijccbs>:
International Journal of Critical Computer-Based Systems or Arima
<https://arima.episciences.org> journal).
