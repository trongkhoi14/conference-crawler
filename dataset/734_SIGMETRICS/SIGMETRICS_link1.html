Welcome to the ACM SIGMETRICS / IFIP Performance 2024 Website!

The organizing committee is pleased to invite you to take part in ACM SIGMETRICS / IFIP Performance 2024 to be held at San Servolo Congress Center, Venice, Italy. SIGMETRICS and Performance are respectively the flagship conferences of the ACM special interest group for the computer systems performance evaluation community and of the IFIP working group WG7.3 on performance modeling and analysis. Every 3 years or so, the two conferences join, and this is the 16th joint conference. We also celebrate the 50th ACM SIGMETRICS conference.
