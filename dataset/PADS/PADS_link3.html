Description

The ACM SIGSIM Conference on Principles of Advanced Discrete Simulation (PADS 2024) is dedicated to all aspects of simulation technology, including:

  • Algorithms and methods for parallel or distributed simulation, including scheduling, synchronization, load balancing and memory management
  • Advanced modeling techniques, including new modeling languages, reuse of models, spatial M&S and agent-based M&S
  • Integration of simulation with other IT systems, methods, and developments including visual analytics, simulation based decision-making, simulation in the grid or in the cloud and intelligent support in M&S
  • New simulation algorithms including adaptive algorithms, hybrid simulation approaches, GPU, and approximations, hybrid architecture acceleration and FPGA
  • M&S applied to solve problems in difficult domains and methodological challenges arising from these applications including real-time and embedded simulation, online and symbiotic simulation and emulation of real systems
  • Mechanisms for efficient design of experiments, including automatic simulation model generation and initialization, dynamic verification and validation of models
  • Tools and techniques for interoperability of simulations, including service-oriented approaches and emerging standards
