Description

The 16th International Conference on Management of Digital EcoSystems (MEDES 2024) is dedicated to the manifold challenges and issues related to web technologies and resource management of Digital Ecosystems.

The 16th International Conference on Management of Digital EcoSystems (MEDES 2024) covers topics such as:

  • Data & Knowledge Management
  • Digital Ecosystem Infrastructure
  • Semantic Computing
  • Computational and Collective Intelligence
  • Big Data
  • Software ecosystems for software engineering
  • Trust, Security & Privacy
  • Services
  • Internet of Things and Intelligent Web
  • Software Engineering
  • Social and Collaborative Platforms
  • Cyber Physical Systems
  • Open Source
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Complex Systems and Networks
  • Applications (Logistics, Energy, Healthcare, Environment, Smart Cities, Digital Humanities, Robotics, etc.)

The 16th International Conference on Management of Digital EcoSystems (MEDES 2024) will be held in Napoli, Italy on 18-20 Nov 2024.
