Description

The ICCAM 2024: International Conference on Computational and Applied Mathematics covers topics such as:

  • Computational physics and chemistry
  • Complex analysis and special functions
  • Constructive techniques for solving ordinary and partial differential equations
  • Computational techniques in engineering
  • Mathematical and Computational Methods in Science and Engineering
  • High Performance Computational Science and Engineering
  • Mathematical programming and optimization
  • Mathematical finance
  • Numerical approximation and interpolation
  • Nonlinear equations
  • Numerical linear algebra
  • Numerical complex analysis
  • Numerical software
  • Numerical quadrature and integral equations
  • Ordinary differential equations
  • Operational research and optimization
  • Partial differential equations
  • Parallel and high performance computing
  • Applications of Computational and Applied Mathematics
  • Quadrature and integral equations

The ICCAM 2024: International Conference on Computational and Applied Mathematics will be held in Amsterdam, Netherlands as well as online on 05-06 Aug 2024.
